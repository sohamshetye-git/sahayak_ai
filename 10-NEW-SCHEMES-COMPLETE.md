## Detailed Scheme Information

### SCH_043 - National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS)
- **Category:** Education
- **Financial Assistance:** ₹12,000 per year
- **Eligibility:** Class 9-12 students, parental income < ₹3.5 Lakhs, 55% marks in Class 8
- **Application:** Online via scholarships.gov.in
- **Status:** Active since 2008

### SCH_044 - Post Matric Scholarship for SC Students
- **Category:** Education  
- **Financial Assistance:** ₹4,560 to ₹14,400 per year
- **Eligibility:** SC students, post-matriculation, parental income < ₹2.5 Lakhs
- **Application:** Online via scholarships.gov.in
- **Status:** Active since 1944

### SCH_047 - Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBC Students
- **Category:** Education
- **Financial Assistance:** ₹3,000 to ₹6,000 per year
- **Eligibility:** OBC students in Class 9-10, parental income < ₹1 Lakh
- **Application:** Online via scholarships.gov.in
- **Status:** Active

### SCH_045 - Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK)
- **Category:** Health
- **Financial Assistance:** Free health services
- **Eligibility:** All children 0-18 years
- **Application:** No application needed - automatic coverage
- **Status:** Active since 2013

### SCH_048 - Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK)
- **Category:** Health
- **Financial Assistance:** Free health services
- **Eligibility:** Adolescents 10-19 years
- **Application:** Available at health facilities
- **Status:** Active since 2014

### SCH_049 - NPCDCS (Cancer, Diabetes, CVD, Stroke Prevention)
- **Category:** Health
- **Financial Assistance:** Free screening and treatment
- **Eligibility:** All citizens above 30 years
- **Application:** Visit nearest NCD clinic
- **Status:** Active

### SCH_046 - Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)
- **Category:** Agriculture
- **Financial Assistance:** ₹50,000 per hectare over 3 years
- **Eligibility:** Farmers in clusters of 50+, organic farming commitment
- **Application:** Through District Agriculture Office
- **Status:** Active since 2015

### SCH_050 - National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)
- **Category:** Agriculture
- **Financial Assistance:** Varies by component (₹10,000 to ₹50,000)
- **Eligibility:** All farmers
- **Application:** Through state agriculture departments
- **Status:** Active since 2014

### SCH_051 - Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
- **Category:** Financial Assistance
- **Financial Assistance:** ₹2 Lakh accident insurance for ₹20/year premium
- **Eligibility:** Age 18-70, bank account holder
- **Application:** Through banks
- **Status:** Active since 2015

### SCH_052 - Stand Up India Scheme
- **Category:** Financial Assistance
- **Financial Assistance:** Loans ₹10 Lakh to ₹1 Crore
- **Eligibility:** SC/ST/Women entrepreneurs
- **Application:** Through banks
- **Status:** Active since 2016
